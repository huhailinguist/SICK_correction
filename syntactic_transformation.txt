Syntactic transformations for MonaLog:

1. a few -> several
EasyCCG and C&C often produce wrong parse for 'a few'.

2. manually give the polarization for 'at most N' and 'at least N'

3. green/blue ... colored -> green/blue

4. a group of -> some

5. a lot of -> much
EasyCCG and C&C often produce wrong parse for 'a lot of'.

6. there is no man doing sth. -> no man is doing sth.

7. passive -> active
